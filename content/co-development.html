Services » Co-Development

Root Software offers co-development partnerships for clients with marketable products.

The idea is simple: If you own a software product that generates at least some regular revenue, Root Software will partner with you as a long-term development team at a discounted rate, in exchange for revenue sharing.

We'll work together to arrange a partnership that's flexible, fair, and utilizes everyone's strengths to build the best software product we can.
