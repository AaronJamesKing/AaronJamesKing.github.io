The Process

  1. Step 1: We'll meet virtually or in person to gain a thorough understanding about your product and the problem it's solving.
  2. Step 2: Next, I'll connect you with a trusted external design team to build a simple and scalable branded design system along with a complete set of wireframes.
  3. Step 3: I'll thoughtfully craft a tech stack specifically for your product. We choose technologies that are proven to be powerful and scalable, instead of whatever just happens to be popular at the moment.
  4. Step 4: Next comes the implementation. Root Software works according to the Agile development process: iteratively, with regular demos and feedback, adjusting as necessary. This process takes around 4-8 weeks.
  5. Step 5: Sweet, sweet delivery. You'll have a fully functioning product, ready to evolve with your business, along with detailed documentation.
