What is Root Software?

We're a network of talented software developers, filling the gaps for companies that don't have the expertise or resources to manage their own custom software. We do software development and maintenance, consulting, and recruiting. See our services page for more details.

How do we do it?

By understanding every client's unique challenges, and tailoring the technical solution and project management accordingly.

Because not every project needs two-week sprints, but some do! And some clients need dynamic hourly development, while others need up-front pricing and scoping. One might need a generalist developer, while another needs a specialized expert. But regardless of your specific situation, you can expect great communication and service along the way.

How can we help?

All you need to do is get in touch! We give out as much free advice as possible. If we aren't the right software partner for you, then we'll try to refer you to an even better match.
