Services » Maintenance and Upgrades

Hopefully somebody told you that software is never finished.

With the constant threat of security vulnerabilities and evolving ecosystems, regular maintenance is crucial.

The Process

  1. Step 1: We'll perform a gap analysis between your software as it stands, and the latest versions of the underlying tech. Deprecated or risky dependencies will be identified along the way.
  2. Step 2: Together, we'll prioritize maintenance tasks based on urgency, effort, risk, and value.
  3. Step 3: Next, we'll help you develop a budget to address the most important tasks without overspending.
  4. Step 4: Root Software will work through the list, with frequent communication and testing.
  5. Step 5: When the project is finished, you'll have safe and stable software along with a guide for future maintenance.
